Brain Fog & Speed / 脑雾与反应速度 [1]

Sleep Apnea (OSA) interrupts your sleep and breathing, which often leads to "brain fog." These tests check how fast your mind reacts and how well you can switch between tasks.

睡眠呼吸暂停 (OSA) 会干扰您的睡眠和呼吸，常导致“脑雾”。这些测验测量您大脑的反应速度以及在不同任务间切换的灵活性。

Recovery / 改善与恢复 [2]

The good news: Treating sleep apnea (like with CPAP) helps clear the fog and restores your mental flexibility as your brain gets the rest it needs.

好消息是：治疗睡眠呼吸暂停（如使用 CPAP）有助于拨开脑雾。随着大脑获得所需的休息，您的思维灵活性会逐渐恢复。

[1] Decary A, et al. Sleep. 2000;23(3):369-381. (Cognitive deficits in sleep apnea sufferers / 睡眠呼吸暂停患者的认知缺陷研究)

[2] Zimmerman ME, et al. Sleep Med Rev. 2011;15(1):19-30. (Recovery of brain function after treatment / 治疗后大脑功能的恢复研究)
